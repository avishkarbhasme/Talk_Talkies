Spider-Man : No Way Home
Peter Parker's life is upended when his identity as Spider-Man is exposed, forcing him to seek Doctor Strange's help to make the world forget. A botched spell tears open the multiverse, bringing villains from other realities into his own. Faced with immense loss, Peter must embrace his destiny, learning the true meaning of sacrifice to save his world and the fabric of reality itself.
Jurassic World
Twenty-two years after the original park's collapse, a new, fully functional dinosaur theme park, Jurassic World, thrives on Isla Nublar. When a genetically modified, highly intelligent dinosaur, the Indominus Rex, escapes its enclosure, chaos erupts across the island. Park operations manager Claire Dearing and raptor trainer Owen Grady must join forces to survive the rampage and save thousands of lives from the unleashed prehistoric predators.
Avengers: Infinity War
The Avengers and their allies must sacrifice everything to try and defeat the powerful Thanos before his blitz of devastation and ruin puts an end to the universe. In order to collect all six Infinity Stones, Thanos plans to inflict his own twisted will on reality. The fate of the planet and existence itself has never been more uncertain as the Avengers have to put it all on the line.

Through this fantastical journey, she discovers her true purpose and helps protect the delicate balance of nature.
